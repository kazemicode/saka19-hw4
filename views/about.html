What is NLP?

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is an intersection of linguistics (the scientific study of human language) and computer science, particularly, computer science. Humans speak languages like Japanese or English; but that is not what computers natively understand. At the most fundamental level, computers communicate in binary. [source]

NLP serves a bridging tool to allow computers and humans to communicate with one another, leveraging one or more human languages.
